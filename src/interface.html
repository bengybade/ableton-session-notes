Lyrics on the timeline

Tag a lyric line with a position, then send it to the timeline as locators or clips.

  • A tagged line starts a block; plain lines under it flow one bar apart.
  • A blank line ends the block, so notes and to-dos are left alone.
  • In View, each lyric shows its spot as a chip (↳ = flows from above).
